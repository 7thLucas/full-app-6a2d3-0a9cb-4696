Foundation Validated

Players feel disconnected from horror games lacking cultural depth and authentic atmosphere

The Problem

Players seeking authentic horror experiences struggle to find games that blend cultural mythology with genuine psychological tension, resulting in forgettable experiences that fail to create lasting dread or emotional investment.

The Solution

Most horror games rely on generic Western tropes and jump scares, missing opportunities to explore rich cultural mythology and create genuinely immersive, emotionally grounded narratives that resonate with diverse audiences.

Target Audience

Horror enthusiasts aged 18-35 who value narrative depth and cultural authenticity

Personal Motivation

To prove that horror games can achieve genuine impact by respecting and celebrating non-Western cultural traditions while delivering unforgettable player experiences
