How to Play

Four steps. One way out.

1

Open the Link

No account. No download. Click the link and you're already inside the bungalow — darkness loads instantly in any browser.

2

Explore the Rooms

Move through the bungalow room by room. Examine objects, find hidden items, and gather clues about where you are — and what's with you.

3

Solve, Unlock, Survive

Use found keys and clues to unlock the next room. Avoid or endure horror events — unpredictable, tense, and designed to keep you on edge.

4

Find the Exit

The bungalow has one way out. Piece together every clue, unlock the final barrier, and escape — if you make it that far.
