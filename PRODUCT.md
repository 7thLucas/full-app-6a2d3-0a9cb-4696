# Horror in the Bangala

## Product Overview
A browser-based horror escape game set inside a dark, haunted bungalow. The player is trapped inside and must explore rooms, find clues and keys, avoid/survive horror elements, and escape through the exit.

## Target Users
Horror game enthusiasts and players who enjoy escape room and survival horror experiences.

## Core Gameplay
- Player starts inside a dark haunted bungalow
- Explore multiple rooms to find clues and keys
- Survive horror elements (ghosts, creatures, jump scares)
- Find the exit and escape to win
- Atmospheric sound cues and visual horror effects

## Brand & Tone
- Dark, eerie, terrifying atmosphere
- Survival horror meets escape room
- Immersive first-person or top-down perspective
- Color scheme: blood red, deep black, eerie greens
